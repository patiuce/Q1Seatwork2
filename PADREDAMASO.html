Father Damaso

Father Damaso is a Spanish friar. He can be described as arrogant, as well as loudmouthed and greedy. Despite being in the Philippines for over twenty years, he can hardly speak or comprehend Tagalog. He forbids that his goddaughter, Maria Clara, to marry Ibarra because of his rivalry with him.
