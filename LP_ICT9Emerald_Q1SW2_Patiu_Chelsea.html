The Characters in NOLI ME TANGERE

Crisostomo Ibarra

Crisostomo Ibarra is a wealthy young man and the son of Don Rafael. He came back from Europe after studying there for seven years and is now in the Philippines. On his first night, General Guevarra told him the truth about his father's death. Because of this, he desires to carry out his father's dreams of building a school.

Maria Clara

Maria Clara is the upper-class daughter of Kapitan Tiago, the wealthiest man in San Diego. She is engaged with Ibarra, albeit Father Damaso is displeased with the arrangement. During a dinner party, Ibarra almost kills Padre Damaso. After that incident, Maria Clara is arranged to marry a Spanish man named Linares despite her hesitation.

Father Damaso

Father Damaso is a Spanish friar known for his arrogance, boisterousness, and greed. Despite being in the Philippines for over twenty years, he can hardly speak or comprehend Tagalog. He forbids his goddaughter, Maria Clara, to marry Ibarra because of their rivalry.
