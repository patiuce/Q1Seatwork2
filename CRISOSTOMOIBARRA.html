Crisostomo Ibarra

Crisostomo Ibarra is a wealthy young man and the son of Don Rafael. He came back from Europe after studying there for seven years, and is now in the Philippines. On his first night, he is told about the truth of his father's death from General Guevarra. Because of this, he soon desires to carry out his father's dreams of building a school.
