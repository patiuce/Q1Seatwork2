Maria Clara

Maria Clara is the upper-class daughter of Kapitan Tiago, the wealthiest man in San Diego. She is engaged with Ibarra, albeit Father Damaso is displeased with the arrangement. During a dinner party, Ibarra is ostracized for almost killing Padre Damaso. After that incident, Maria Clara is arranged to marry a Spanish man named Linares despite her hesitation.
